## Consent

By using this website, you hereby give your consent to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information as described in the "Information we collect" and "How we use your Information" sections.

## Information we collect

We may collect personal identification information from Users in various ways, including when Users visit our Website, register on the Website, place an order, fill out a form, respond to a survey, or engage in other activities or resources available on our Website. The information collected may include name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment information. Users may visit our Website anonymously, and we only collect personal identification information if it is voluntarily submitted by Users. However, please note that refusing to provide certain information may prevent Users from engaging in specific Website-related activities.

## How we use your Information

We may collect and use Users' personal information for the following purposes:

- **Improving customer service:** The information you provide helps us respond to your customer service requests and support needs more efficiently.
- **Personalizing user experience:** We may use aggregated information to understand how Users, as a group, utilize the services and resources on our Website.
- **Enhancing our Website:** User feedback allows us to improve our products and services.
- **Processing payments:** Information provided by Users during the ordering process is used solely to facilitate that specific order and is not shared with external parties unless necessary for order fulfillment.
- **Running promotions, contests, surveys, and other features:** Users may receive information they agreed to receive regarding topics of interest.
- **Sending periodic emails:** The provided email address may be used to send User information and updates pertaining to their order. It may also be utilized to respond to inquiries, questions, and other requests.
